What is ALx About?

This write up talks about ALX vision, mission and values.

Mission

The ALX MiSsion is to promote a peaceful and prosperous Africa through developing and connecting transformational and impact oriented leaders across the continent.

Vision

The vision of ALx is to develop and connect 3 million ethical entrepreneurial leaders by 2035 who will innovatively come up with solutions for Africa's grand challenges.

Values

ALX founding values are as follows

  • Courage
  • Humility
  • Adventure
  • Initiative
  • Resilient

ALX is the new era of unlocking young leaders' potential!
